# AI Agents Specification

The Akram AI System includes specialized agents.

## Research Agent

Role
Market research and opportunity discovery.

Responsibilities
- analyze markets
- identify trends
- study competitors
- evaluate opportunities

---

## Business Agent

Role
Business strategy and monetization.

Responsibilities
- design business models
- pricing strategies
- revenue analysis
- product positioning

---

## Automation Agent

Role
Automation architecture.

Responsibilities
- design n8n workflows
- automate operations
- connect APIs and tools

---

## Creative Agent

Role
Branding and marketing strategy.

Responsibilities
- brand positioning
- product naming
- marketing concepts
